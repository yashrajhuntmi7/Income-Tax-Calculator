Income Tax Calculator

Calculate your income tax easily for both Old and New Tax Regimes. Enter your annual income, deductions, and age group to instantly see your tax liability. The calculator can also suggest which regime saves you more money.

EMI Calculator

Estimate your monthly loan repayments with ease. Input your loan amount, interest rate, and tenure, and the calculator provides your EMI along with total interest payable. Perfect for planning home, car, or personal loans.

Plan your savings to achieve your goals. Enter your target amount, monthly contribution, or time horizon, and see how long it will take to reach your goal. Helps you save smarter and stay on track financially.

Currency Converter

Convert currencies quickly with up-to-date rates. Select the source and target currencies, enter the amount, and get the converted value instantly. Ideal for travelers, freelancers, and international shoppers.
